# Ayurser — Roadmap

*The destination is a living health journal, inspired by Ayurveda — built around one
protected minute: "How am I, really?" Every phase serves that minute. See `PHILOSOPHY.md`.*

The order is deliberate: prove the minute, then deepen the knowledge, then bring in
humans, then — only once trusted — open the marketplace. We do not skip ahead. We do
not build toward a later phase until the one before it has earned the right.

---

## Phase 0 — Foundation (this week)
The real ground. Nothing clever.
- Logo · brand · a minimal design system (tokens, not a cathedral)
- GitHub · Next.js · Supabase · Vercel

*Why now:* the learning vision needs **memory**. A living journal cannot live in
localStorage. The soul promoted the architecture — Supabase is no longer "later," it is
the prerequisite.

## Phase 1 — The minute, live
- Daily check-in · the **qualities** engine (*opposites pacify*, AH Su. 1.13½) · result ·
  **one** recommendation
- Persisted from the first morning — *capture early, interpret later*
- Ship it.

> **This week, in practice:** Phase 0 and the first breath of Phase 1 fold together —
> stand up the real foundation **and** get the qualities check-in writing one honest
> result to Supabase. End the week with the minute *live and remembering*: something to
> actually feel, even before it interprets what it remembers.

## Phase 2 — Knowledge
Foods · rituals · breathing · yoga · herbs. Drawn from `knowledge/`, cited, taught
just-in-time — never a wall of text.

## Phase 3 — Practitioners
Practitioner profiles · directory · booking. *Human at the top*, made real.

## Phase 4 — The practitioner's side
Practitioner dashboard · questionnaires · patient notes · photos · recommendations.

## Phase 5 — The evolving artwork
The living tree (see `IDEAS.md`) — grows only, never shames, paced by check-ins.
*"This is where the app starts becoming memorable."*

## Phase 6 — Marketplace
Only after people trust you. Not a centimetre sooner.

---

## Discipline
- **Phases 3–6 are horizon, not commitment.** Signal-gated — real users, Vanessa's
  survey, demand you can see. Don't build toward them until the minute has earned a
  single person's tomorrow.
- **The minute is sacred.** However sophisticated the engine grows, the experience stays
  calm, simple, effortless. Protected from day one.
- **The bones are portable.** The qualities → opposites engine drafted in conversation
  survives the replatform to Next.js untouched. Nothing is wasted in moving stacks.
