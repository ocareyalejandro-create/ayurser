# Ayurser — Ideas (considered, not yet built)

A parking lot for design ideas we've reasoned through but haven't built.
Each must answer to `PHILOSOPHY.md` before it ships.

## The living tree (ambient progress)
An evolving artwork that grows with the person: seed → sprout → roots → growth →
alignment → full bloom — ending as the Ayurser tree-of-life logo (the user grows
*into* the brand mark).
- **Grows only** — never wilts, never resets, never shames. A record of presence,
  not a streak. Miss a month, the tree waits where you left it; the next check-in
  nudges it on.
- **Paced by check-ins, not calendar days** (matches discovery milestones ~7, ~21–30).
- **No finish line** — "full bloom" is a living fullness with seasons, not "arrived."
- Presence, not gamification. Witnessed, not earned.

## The moon (optional, cycle-aware)
For people who choose to share a cycle: a small moon reflecting their phase
(waxing / full / waning), and guidance that softens or lifts to match — Vata at
menstruation, Kapha building, Pitta premenstrual (see `knowledge/ayurveda.md`).
- **Optional & considerate:** ask *"do you track a cycle?"*, never assume; skippable.
- **Appears for those it serves, simply absent otherwise** — never a "women only" wall.
- **Poetic, not clinical** — a moon, not a hormone chart. (O'Donnell: cycle phases
  echo the moon's.)
- **Pairs with the tree:** tree = your journey over time, moon = your month. Two calm,
  living, ambient reflections. Same family — witnessed, never gamified.

## The opt-in "learn the basics" card (after first results)
After the first check-in's results, one quiet card: *"Curious why we asked those
questions? — Learn in 2 min / Maybe later."* Tap → the 3 intro screens (wind/fire/
earth · nature-vs-weather · the promise). Respects the curious *and* the just-trying.
Education pulled, never pushed. (Intro copy parked in `knowledge/learn-copy.md`.)

## Considered cycle question
If asked at all: *"Do you track a menstrual cycle?"* — the relevant physiological
fact, optional, never *"are you a woman?"*
