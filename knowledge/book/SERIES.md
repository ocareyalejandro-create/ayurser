# The Ayurser Series — a roadmap

*A shelf, not a single book. We write one at a time, finish it, wire it into the
app, and let it reach real readers before opening the next. Dream the series;
ship the book.*

---

## The Ayurveda series — five books

### Book One · The Foundations — *drafted*
The language and the laws. The five elements; the qualities you can feel; the six
tastes; the three forces (Vāta, Pitta, Kapha); the digestive fire; living in
rhythm with the day and the year; what the body is made of; and the mind.
Everything else stands on this.

### Book Two · The Table — *next*
Food as medicine, in full. Eating for your constitution and today's weather; the
shape of a day's meals; cooking warm and simple; seasonal eating; spices and the
kitchen herbs; gentle cleansing and fasting. With recipes and a larder.

### Book Three · The Practice — *illustrated*
The body and the breath. Yoga āsana chosen by constitution; prāṇāyāma; meditation
and yoga nidrā; the full daily routine; rest and rejuvenation. Each practice
plainly described and **beautifully illustrated in our own hand** — the craft at
the heart of the series.

### Book Four · The Seasons of a Life
Care that changes as a life does: the monthly cycle and women's health; pregnancy
and the time after; the child; the elder; the ages and stages, each with its own
needs.

### Book Five · The Living Body & the Wider World
The deeper and the subtle: the tissues, channels, and ojas in depth; prāṇa and
the subtle body; the mind and meditation, further in; and *ecology and
relationship* — living in tune not only with yourself, but with others and the
earth.

---

## Beyond Ayurveda — the far horizon

Each living tradition could one day be its own shelf — kept as **separate lenses,
never blended**. That is a horizon, not a plan. We earn the right to a second
tradition by doing the first one completely and honestly.

## The discipline

One book at a time. Finish it, wire it into the app, let it find real readers —
then open the next. Depth is the work, and the work is the moat.
