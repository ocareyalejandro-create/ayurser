# The Ayurser Series — a roadmap

*A shelf, not a single book. We write one at a time, finish it, wire it into the
app, and let it reach real readers before opening the next. Dream the series;
ship the book.*

The whole series is **designed** below — every book's contents. Only Book One is
**written**. Books Two–Five are drafted properly, one at a time, at the depth and
honesty of the first.

---

## Book One · The Foundations — *written ✅*

The language and the laws.

- Author's Note · Introduction — *What Ayurveda Is*
- **The Language of Nature** — The Five Elements · The Qualities · The Six Tastes

- **The Fire** — Agni
- **Living in Rhythm** — The Day · The Seasons · Sleep · Oil
- **What We Are Made Of** — The Tissues · The Channels · Ojas
- **The Mind** — The Three States · The Senses

## Beyond Ayurveda — the far horizon

Each living tradition could one day be its own shelf — kept as **separate lenses,
never blended**. A horizon, not a plan. We earn the right to a second tradition by
doing the first one completely and honestly.

## The discipline

One book at a time. Finish it, wire it into the app, let it find real readers —
then open the next. Depth is the work, and the work is the moat.
