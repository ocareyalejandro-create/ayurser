## The one line
**"Your nature is constant. Your needs are not."**
(The whole Prakriti/Vikriti relationship, no Sanskrit required.)

## The three energies (balanced/gift expression — for an inviting tone)
- **Vata** — like the wind: creative, light and always moving.
- **Pitta** — like fire: focused, warm and full of drive.
- **Kapha** — like the earth: steady, grounded and calm.
Together we call them the doshas. You're a blend of all three, with one or two
that tend to lead. *(Their imbalances — dryness, heat, heaviness — show up in the
daily check-in, not here.)*

## Nature vs today (Prakriti / Vikriti)
Your nature doesn't change overnight. Ayurveda calls this your **Prakriti** — your
natural constitution. But each day is different: sleep, food, stress and the
seasons all shape how you feel today. Ayurveda calls this your **Vikriti**.
We check in with your Vikriti, and over time we'll come to know your nature together.

## The promise
No questionnaire can truly know you. By checking in, day by day, we begin to
recognise your patterns and help you build small, healthier habits.
For deeper guidance, a practitioner can see what an app can't.
