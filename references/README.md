# references/ — local only

Raw Ayurveda / yoga source texts (PDF, epub) live here on Alex's machine.

**These are gitignored and never pushed.** Reasons:
- The repo is public; most of these are copyrighted.
- They bloat the repo.

What gets committed instead is `../knowledge/ayurveda.md` — our own distilled,
**attributed** notes drawn from these sources. We cite; we do not redistribute.

Ancient roots (Charaka Samhita, Sushruta Samhita, Ashtanga Hridaya) in
public-domain translation may be quoted freely. Modern authors (Lad, Frawley,
etc.) are referenced, never copied.
