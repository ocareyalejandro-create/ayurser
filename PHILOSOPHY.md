# Ayurser — Philosophy

*This is not a feature list. It is the stance every decision answers to.*
*When a choice over-claims, rushes, or clutters — this file says no.*

---

## What we are

**Ayurveda, made reachable.** Honest daily guidance, and real practitioners when it matters.

- **Reachable** — not gatekept behind jargon, expense, or mysticism.
- **Honest** — it admits what it doesn't know.
- **Human at the top** — the app guides; practitioners hold the authority.
  *Human practitioners > AI authority.* Always.

---

## Weather, not labels

You are a mix of three doshas, with one that tends to lead.

- **Prakriti is your nature.** Hard to pin — and we don't pretend a quiz can.
- **Vikriti is your weather.** What your body is asking for *today*.

We lead with the **weather**, not the label. Ayurveda is about *dynamic balance*,
not fixed boxes. "Here is what you need today" is more honest, more useful, and
more truly Ayurvedic than "here is your permanent type."

---

## Honesty is the edge

The app does not pretend to know you in sixty seconds. **It listens daily, and
discovers you slowly.** That humility is the whole differentiator — and it
dissolves the trap every other app falls into: selling a final verdict off a
one-minute form. We stop pretending the verdict is final, and the product gets
*more* trustworthy, not less.

---

## How engagement actually works

- **The dosha reveal is a hook, not a habit.** "Here's your type" engages once,
  then it's spent. Why return to a label you already have? Don't over-invest here.
- **The daily routine is the habit** — the felt usefulness, the small ritual,
  the "I feel a bit better when I do this." That earns a tomorrow.
- **The slow discovery is the return-engine** — *"check in again and I'll
  understand you a little better"* is a genuine reason to come back, woven into
  the honesty itself.

## Discovery, paced

Never leave someone empty-handed, never over-claim.

- **Day one:** a soft *first impression* — a leaning, not a verdict.
- **~7 check-ins:** "your first pattern is forming."
- **~21–30 check-ins:** "a fuller picture of your tendencies."
- A breadcrumb each week, not a six-week silence.

**Caveat we always keep:** daily check-ins measure *today's weather* (Vikriti).
Constitution (Prakriti) is **not** the average of your bad days — repeated
imbalance can be season or lifestyle, not nature. So what emerges is framed as
**"your tendencies — what tends to pull you off balance,"** which is both
truthful and, frankly, more useful than a fixed label.

---

## Design restraint

- **Two ideas, not ten.** Teach *nature vs. weather*; let words like *agni*
  arrive later, in context. Depth lives in "Learn," not the front door.
- **Guidance > Information.** No walls of text.
- **Calm comes from restraint** — two fonts, one accent, lots of space.

---

## The lines we do not cross

- We do not diagnose. *Lifestyle guidance inspired by Ayurveda — not medical advice.*
- We do not sell a final verdict.
- We do not redistribute others' work; we cite it. (See `knowledge/`.)
- We do not put AI above a qualified human.
